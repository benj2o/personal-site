Entrepreneur First was a useful reminder that founder judgment often shows up under pressure. You meet fast, decide fast, and learn very quickly whether an idea, a person, or a conversation has any real pull.

I like that environment. It rewards people who can move before they have perfect information, while still being honest enough to update when they are wrong.

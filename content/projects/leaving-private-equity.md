Private equity is extremely good at training a certain kind of mind. You learn to be precise, skeptical, and fast with numbers. You also learn how much of the world can be flattened into assumptions.

I still like analysis. I just do not want it to end inside a model. I want the work closer to things that can be built, tested, broken, and made real.
